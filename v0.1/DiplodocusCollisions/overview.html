DiplodocusCollisions.jl ​

DiplodocusCollisions.jl hosts all the functionality required to generate collision matrices for both emissive and binary particle interactions. These matrices can the by used by DiplodocusTransport.jl to evolve particle distribution functions according to these interactions.
